Welcome to Earth

Earth is the third planet in the Solar System and also our home. It is the only known habitable planet in the Solar System that can support human like life forms. Earth is believed to have formed over 4.5 billion years ago. Life came to be in the form of early anaerobic and subsequent aerobic organisms. Earth has a satellite that orbits it, called the Moon.
